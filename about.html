The Spark: Why We Started

EcoClean began with a simple frustration: seeing pollution choke our local rivers and parks. In **2018**, a small group of friends decided to stop complaining and start cleaning. What began as a single community cleanup event quickly grew into a movement, fueled by the passion of local volunteers.

Our goal was never just to pick up trash; it was to **shift mindsets**. We wanted to raise awareness that every person, every community, holds the power to restore our planet's natural beauty. We use education, organization, and a little bit of fun to make environmental action accessible to everyone.

Our Promise to the Planet

We believe in transparent, measurable results. Every piece of litter collected is tracked, and every volunteer hour is valued. Our efforts focus not just on physical cleaning, but on promoting sustainable habits like proper recycling, reducing single-use plastics, and advocating for cleaner local policies. Our commitment is to continuous improvement and lasting change.
